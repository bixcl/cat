  • The Sphynx cat is one of the rare species, and it is the cat that does not have hair and is known as the bald Canadian cat, as it is called in some countries as the Pharaonic cat.

  • Information about the pharaonic cat

First point

Pharaonic cats are hairless cats of medium size, and the weight of an adult cat ranges from approximately 3 to 6 kilograms.

Point two

The pharaonic cat has a small head, large eyes, and large pointed ears. They live approximately 8 to 14 years.

point three

Their body temperature is a degree or two higher than the average body temperature of other types of cats. They are also more voracious, which I attribute to their need to replace lost heat through their skin.

point four

The Pharaonic cat is more sensitive than other species to cold, as a result of the absence of the dense fur layer responsible for keeping it warm during the cold seasons, although the increased fatty secretion in the skin of this cat slightly compensates for the absence of fur. However, it still needs special care and constant heating during these seasons. In addition, the outer fatty layer increases the cat's pick-up of dirt, and therefore, it needs continuous cleaning at a regular rate (weekly or monthly).

point five

Some believe that the absence of thick fur on these cats makes them non-allergic (or safe for allergy sufferers), but this is not true. As the Pharaonic cats shed some of their skin cells from time to time, and this may cause some allergies, in addition to the sensitivity that the small, thin hairs on their skin may cause.

Information about the personality of the Pharaonic cats


Pharaonic cats may seem scary to some, but in reality they are cats that love people and interact with them, are demanding and love attention, and the Pharaonic cat gets very excited when its owner returns home and tries to distract him while doing his daily work.

Pharaonic cat food

    • As for the food of the Pharaonic cats, they love food very much, and they eat anything that is offered to them. Caution and taking care of their food is important, due to the high rates of metabolism in this type of cat, and the severity of the sensitivity of their digestive system. It is advised to feed them small meals at frequent intervals during the day.

  • Cat food that is sold in pharmacies contains the nutritional values ​​required to maintain their health. It is advised to add canned foods and fish such as tuna and sardines to the list of their diet, and do not forget that these cats lose a lot of water by evaporation through their smooth skin, and therefore they need water permanently to prevent dehydration.
